About Us

Serving Itahari Since 1987

Itahari Government Hospital is a publicly funded healthcare institution located in Itahari, Sunsari, Nepal. Established to provide accessible and affordable medical care to all residents of the region, the hospital operates under the Government of Nepal's health ministry.

We believe that quality healthcare is a right, not a privilege. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every patient receives compassionate, professional care regardless of their financial background.

We proudly accept Bima (Health Insurance) for eligible patients.

  • ✅ Affordable healthcare services for all
  • ✅ Government-supported and publicly funded
  • ✅ Bima / Health Insurance fully accepted
  • ✅ Emergency care available 24 hours, 7 days a week
  • ✅ Qualified doctors and certified medical staff
  • ✅ On-site laboratory and pharmacy services
  • ✅ Maternity and child care facilities
  • ✅ Specialist referral services available

Health Insurance

Bima / Health Insurance Service

Itahari Government Hospital is proud to be a Bima-registered hospital. Eligible patients can access medical treatment using their government health insurance card. We fully support the national health insurance programme.

How Bima Works Here

  • 🛡️ Bima service is available for OPD consultations
  • 🛡️ Laboratory and diagnostic tests are covered
  • 🛡️ Prescribed medicines from pharmacy are included
  • 🛡️ Selected surgical and inpatient treatments covered
  • 🛡️ Maternity and child care services are eligible
  • 📄 Bring your Bima card, citizenship card, and referral documents
  • 📄 Insurance must be active and valid on the date of visit
  • 📄 Go to the Bima counter at reception before consulting a doctor
